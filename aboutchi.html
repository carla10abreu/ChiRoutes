About Us





We are a group of five girls who developed ChiRoutes.
We are from all over the Chicagoland area and met in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Microsoft.
ChiRoutes was our final project.
Our goal is to make safer routes accesible to young people in Chicago.

About Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a national non-profit organization dedicated to closing the gender gap in STEM. There are clubs you can join or start in/for your community. For rising junior and senior girls in high school, Girls Who Code hosts Summer Immersion Programs in multiple cities at multiple tech companies.
